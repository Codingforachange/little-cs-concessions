Meet the Owners

Conrad & Angelica Hoover

Founded in 2021, Little C’s Concessions is the passion project of U.S. Army Veteran Conrad Hoover and his wife, Angelica. What started as a love for classic fairground treats has grown into a community staple specializing in high-energy "fun foods."

Where You'll Find Us:
  • Festivals & Fairs
  • Carnival Services
  • Fair Grounds
  • Corporate Events
  • Fundraising
  • Birthday Parties

"We bring the carnival to you—with the dedication and pride of a Veteran-owned family business."
